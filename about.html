Our Marysville, WA Dental Team

DR. JUNG LEE

When he was a child, Dr. Jung Lee’s family moved from Korea to Washington, where he has lived ever since. After obtaining his bachelor’s degree in biochemistry from the University of Washington, he attended the university’s dental school, where he earned his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree.

A lack of proper oral care during his childhood led a young Dr. Lee to spend a lot of time in dental offices throughout his teenage years. His positive dental experiences inspired him to become a dentist himself. They also gave him the ability to understand dental appointments from a patient’s perspective and to create an atmosphere that is warm, comforting, and personal. Dr. Lee loves speaking with his patients about their oral health, educating them on proper care and treatments, and providing them with the individualized care they need and deserve.
